protection of cows


We provide protection and shelter for cow safety



Gauseva, meaning "service to cows," is an SRM College initiative dedicated to the care and well-being of cows. It provides shelter, nourishment, and medical care to stray or injured cows while promoting awareness about cow protection. Gauseva fosters compassion, responsibility, and environmental stewardship, encouraging the community to uphold the values of respect for life and sustainability. .
